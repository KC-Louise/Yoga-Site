About Us

After becoming acquainted in college, we decided to venture on a spiritual, mental and physical journey to relieve the stress of college life. We each began practicing and studying various yoga practices and eventually decicded to share our knowledge with those who may also want to better their wellbeing. Satya, loosely translated from Sanskrit as "truth" is what our practice embodies wholly.
